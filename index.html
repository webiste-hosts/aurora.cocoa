Our Story

"Aurora Cocoa is born from a simple belief: that chocolate is not just a sweet treat, but a sensory experience that should be crafted with raw passion."

We take the finest single-origin dark cocoa beans, roasting and stone-grinding them in small batches. Our tempering process is entirely hand-guided on marble slabs, ensuring that crisp snap and glossy sheen that distinguishes premium chocolate.

From whole roasted almonds and crushed cookies to exotic fruit-and-nut medleys or traditional gulkand inclusions, every ingredient is selected to create a harmonious blend of textures and depth.
